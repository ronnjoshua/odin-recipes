Buttered Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 1 sachet, MAGGI Magic Sarap 8g
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 head garlic
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 0.25 cup banana catsup
  • 1 tbsp spring onion

Cooking Instructions

  1. Season shrimp with MAGGI Magic Sarap for 2 mins.
  2. Saute shrimp in 2 tbsp of butter over high heat for 1 minute. Remove and set for 3 minutes.
  3. Melt remaining butter in the same pan and saute garlic. Add sugar, catsup and sauteed shrimp for 5 minutes.
  4. Transfer on a serving plate and garnish with spring onion. Enjoy!
